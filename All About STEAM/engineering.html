“The branch of science and technology concerned with the design, building, and use of engines, machines, and structures. The art or science of making practical application of the knowledge of pure sciences."

It is the practical solution to almost any kind of problem which holds the capacity to settle, prevent and preview any practical anomaly in an object. Therefore engineering is the answer to any predicament regarding scientific and technical working of tools.

Design
It is an iterative process used to identify problems and develop and improve solutions. It also can be extremely useful to any individual trying to solve a problem. But engineering design can be – and usually is – done in teams. Each team member brings different knowledge and experience to the process, which usually improves the results.
Enhance
With the help of technology, knowledge in engineering can be improved further while enhancing ideas and creativity on solving problems and completing tasks.
Plan
To accurately and unambiguously capture all the geometric features of a site, building, product or component. The goal is to portray or convey enough information to allow people to realize a design
Construct
To construct dreams and best solutions which eases and improve on more problems and creativity of ones idea.

Benefits of Engineering

Engineering disciplines integrate scientific principles with practically oriented research, providing systems and processes that themselves create ways of acquiring new knowledge. This integration makes engineering critical to successful industrial innovation.
