Measure
It is a generalisation of the concepts as length, area and volume. Informally, measurement may be regarded as "mass distributions". More precisely, a measurement is a function that assigns a number to certain subsets of a given set. This number is said to be the measure of the set.
Formulate
After measuring, definitely we create or prepare methodically, or express the idea in a concise or systematic way.
Quantify
It is the act of counting and measuring that maps human sense observations and experiences into members of some set of numbers.
Compute
When you solve a mathematical problem, you compute the answer. To compute is to calculate, either literally or figuratively.

Benefits of Mathematics

No matter which way we look at it, mathematics comes first as the core. Solid foundation in mathematics are essential to the practitioners in science, technology and engineering. Studying mathematics will not only develop more engineers and scientists, but also develop citizens to be more critical in their chosen careers/ fields. The workforce of tomorrow, in a number of fields, will demand mathematics.
